vision and mission

our vision

Palestine Technical University “Kadoorie” looks forward to becoming a leading public university and a hub for scientific research, dissemination of knowledge and a center for quality higher education while contributing to serving the Palestinian community and actively engaged in its sustainable development.

our mission

Palestine Technical University “Khadouri” provides distinctive and carefully selected technical educational programs at different levels, spanning from professional diploma to master degree programs and beyond. To achieve its objectives, the university has a distinguished and diverse faculties committed to improving the teaching process, producing innovative scientific and technical research related to local and regional challenges as well as creating a stimulating environment for teaching, learning and intellectual creativity with optimal use of technology. The university also encourages and supports effective local and global partnership with other educational and research institutions.
